Particle Gravity

ParticleGravity.java

A particle Game where each particle moves towards the mouse. The closer the particle gets the faster it moves.

Game of Life

GameOfLife.java

An implementation of Conway's Game of Life. The rules that decide which cell is white or black can be found in the Conway's Game of Life Wikipedia article. This implementation also let's the user black cells by clicking on a white cell and moving the mouse or setting white cells by clicking on a black cell and moving the mouse.
