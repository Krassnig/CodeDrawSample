Game of Life

Download GameOfLife.java CodeDraw Library

An implementation of Conway's Game of Life. The rules that decide which cells are white or black can be found in the Conway's Game of Life Wikipedia article. This implementation also let's the user place black cells by clicking on a white cell and moving the mouse and place white cells by clicking on a black cell and moving the mouse.
